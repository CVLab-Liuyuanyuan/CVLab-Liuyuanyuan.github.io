Research Area

The Comper vision and emotion computing lab (CVEC-Lab) focuses on the following topics:

  1. Face analysis including head pose estimation, facial expression recognition, face detection, and facial feature localization in unconstrained environment
  2. Scene recognition and understanding including scene recognition, object detection, and semantic segmentation
  3. Human computer interface including engagement study and multi-person visual focus of attention recognition
